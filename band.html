Sugarland

Band Members

Jennifer Nettles - lead vocals
Kristian Bush - vocals, mandolin, acoustic guitar and harmonica

Albums

  • Enjoy the Ride

    Previously a trio, founding member Kristen Hall left in 2005. In 2016, Sugarland released "Enjoy the Ride" where it opened at #4 on the US Top 200 and #2 on the Top Country charts. The album became multi-platinum status after selling 3 million copies.

  • Love on the Inside

    The duo's first album featuring a deluxe fan edition. This album produced three chart singles: "All I Want to Do", "Already Gone", and "It Happens". Selling over 2 million copies in the United States, "Enjoy the Ride" was the first album in their career to reach #1 on Top Country Albums and The Billboard Top 200 charts.

  • The Incredible Machine

    Released in October 2010, the album received varying opinions from critics. The album distanced Sugarland as it was more rock than traditional country. Promotion was tied with the duo's "The Incredible Machine Tour". Commercially, the album sold well. It debuted at #1 on the U.S. Billboard 200 and Top Country Album charts.
